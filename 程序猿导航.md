LPT: Save time by performing as many morning tasks as you can the night before.Productivity
<br/>
Most people are groggy and perform tasks more slowly just after waking, but move much more quickly at night. Many think to lay their clothes out, pack lunches, or shower at night, but even smaller things add up. Going to the gym? Place your keys, wallet, towel etc in your gym bag. Pre-mix your preworkout or protein. Taking the kids to school? Place lunches and all materials in their bags. Lay out their clothes as well. Breakfast? Precook everything at night and lay out the needed silverware and cups. Load and preset the coffee maker to automatically brew. Taking away as many minute steps from your morning will add up to big time savings.
